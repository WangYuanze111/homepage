#概率论 #信息论 #编码原理

预备知识

符号约定

  • 大写字母\(X , Y , Z\) 等表示随机变量
  • 小写字母\(x_i , y_j,x_1,x_n,y_m\)表示随机变量的具体取值，即为随机事件。

概率空间

离散随机变量\(X\)的概率空间为： \[ \begin{bmatrix} X\P(X)\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} x_1 &x_2 & \cdots & x_n\ y_1 &y_2 & \cdots & y_m\end{bmatrix},\ \sum_{i=1}^np(x_i) = 1 \]

\[ \begin{array}{l} P_{X}=\left[\begin{array}{llll} p\left(x_{1}\right) & p\left(x_{2}\right) & \cdots & p\left(x_{n}\right) \end{array}\right] \ =\left[\begin{array}{lllll} p_{x_{1}} & p_{x_{2}} & \cdots & p_{x_{n}} \end{array}\right]=\left[\begin{array}{llll} p_{1} & p_{2} & \cdots & p_{n} \end{array}\right] \end{array} \]

离散随机变量$Y$的概率空间为： n替换为m即可，和X相同

离散随机变量的联合概率空间

$$ P_{X Y}=\begin{array}{c} x_{1} \ x_{2} \ \vdots \ x_{n} \end{array}\left[\begin{array}{cccc} p\left(x_{1} y_{1}\right) & p\left(x_{1} y_{2}\right) & \cdots & p\left(x_{1} y_{m}\right) \ p\left(x_{2} y_{1}\right) & p\left(x_{2} y_{2}\right) & \cdots & p\left(x_{2} y_{m}\right) \ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots \ p\left(x_{n} y_{1}\right) & p\left(x_{n} y_{2}\right) & \cdots & p\left(x_{n} y_{m}\right) \end{array}\right] $$

全概率公式

$$ p\left(y_{j}\right)=\sum_{i=1}^{n} p\left(x_{i}\right) p\left(y_{j} \mid x_{i}\right)=\sum_{i=1}^{n} p\left(x_{i} y_{j}\right) $$

贝叶斯公式

$$ p\left(x_{i} \mid y_{j}\right)=\frac{p\left(x_{i} y_{j}\right)}{p\left(y_{j}\right)}=\frac{p\left(x_{i}\right) p\left(y_{j} \mid x_{i}\right)}{\sum_{i=1}^{n} p\left(x_{i}\right) p\left(y_{j} \mid x_{i}\right)} $$
